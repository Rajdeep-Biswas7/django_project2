Service FAQs

❓ How do subscription boxes work?

Choose your subscription tier, and we'll deliver a curated selection of teas to your door each month. You can pause or cancel anytime.

❓ What's included in the tasting session?

Sample teas, expert guidance on brewing and tasting notes, food pairing suggestions, and a personalized recommendation list.

❓ Can I customize bulk orders?

Absolutely! We offer custom blends, packaging designs, and labeling options. Contact our B2B team for details.

❓ How far in advance for event planning?

We recommend booking at least 4 weeks in advance for custom events, but we'll do our best to accommodate shorter timelines.

❓ Are there group discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for group sessions, corporate teams, and bulk purchases. Contact us for a custom quote.

❓ Do you offer refunds if unsatisfied?

Yes! We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all services. If you're unhappy, we'll work to make it right.
